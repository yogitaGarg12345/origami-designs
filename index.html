Interesting facts about camel

  1. Camel is also known as the 'ship of the desert'
  2. Camel can live without food and water for long time
  3. camel can walk easily in sand due to it's special feet

Interesting facts about chameleons

  • chameleons are reptiles
  • chameleons can change their colour which help them to escape from hunters
  • chameleons have prehensile tail which help them to wrap themselves around branches of trees

Interesting facts about cicada

  1. All cicada species in North America came from a common ancestor
  2. cicada species switches from being on a 13-year cycle to a 17-year cycle

Interesting facts about panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day

Interesting facts about pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about teddy

  2. Winnie the Pooh was based on a real bear
